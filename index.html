About Us


At Super Subs we are dedicated to serving you the best sub sandwiches you've ever had. Our mission is to create an excellent dining experience that keeps you coming back for more. With passion for flavour and commitment t serving high quality products, we strive to be your go-to for satisfying sub sandwiches.

What truly sets Super Subs apart is our dedication to customer satisfaction. We believe in providing exceptional service and treating each customer like a VIP. From the moment you step into our welcoming atmosphere, you'll be greeted by our friendly staff who are ready to assist you in creating the perfect sub. We value your preferences and dietary needs, offering a wide variety of options.

Join us at Super Subs and embark on the sandwich adventure of your dreams! We invite you to explore our diverse menu,featuring classic favorites and unique customer creations that are sure to delight your senses. Whether you're craving a traditional sub or seeking an interesting flavor combination, we have something to satisfy every craving. Indulge in the ultimate sub sandwich experience at Super Subs!
